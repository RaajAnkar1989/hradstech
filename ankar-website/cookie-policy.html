Cookie Policy

Understanding our cookie usage

Last updated: January 15, 2025

Quick Summary: This Cookie Policy explains how HRADSTECH SOLUTIONS uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and POS software. We use cookies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and improve our services.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit our website or use our POS software. They help us remember your preferences and provide you with a better experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • Essential Functions: To ensure our website and software work properly
  • User Experience: To remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics: To understand how you use our services and improve them
  • Security: To protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Marketing: To show you relevant content and advertisements

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for our website and software to function properly. They cannot be disabled and are set in response to actions you take, such as logging in or setting privacy preferences.

3.2 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your language preference or login status.

3.3 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website and software by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

3.4 Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to track visitors across websites to display relevant and engaging advertisements.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics and performance monitoring
  • Payment Processors: For secure payment processing
  • Customer Support: For live chat and support functionality
  • Social Media: For social media integration and sharing

These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie practices. We recommend reviewing their policies for more information.

7. Browser Settings

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Receive notifications when cookies are set

Browser-Specific Instructions:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

8. Mobile Devices

On mobile devices, you can control cookies through your browser settings or by using the privacy settings in your device's operating system. Some mobile apps may also have their own cookie controls.

9. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies, some features of our website and POS software may not work properly:

  • You may need to log in repeatedly
  • Your preferences may not be saved
  • Some features may be unavailable
  • Performance may be affected

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website.
